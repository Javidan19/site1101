Project 1

How Does Hardware Work?

This project was completed as part of the course Fall 2025 – Principles of Information Systems (SITE-1101). The main purpose of the project was to understand how computer hardware processes information using logic gates.

In this project, we explored how basic logic gates operate and how they form the foundation of digital systems. Logic gates are essential components of computer hardware and are responsible for performing logical operations.

Topics Covered

  • NOT, AND, OR logic gates
  • XOR gate (bonus task)
  • NAND gate (bonus task)
  • Basic principles of digital hardware
